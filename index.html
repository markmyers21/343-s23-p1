DD FC

Duke Dynasty Football Club

This is the official website of DD FC. DD FC is an intramural soccer team at James Madison University. They play at each opportunity they get in every semester of the sport. This includes teams times in the fall and spring for outdoor as well as the winter for indoor.

News

DD FC are now in the off season until after spring break. However, they will pick up again on March 26th, 2023 for the outdoor season. Their last game was an indoor playoff game against Sand Frijoleros. The game finished 4-2 after DD FC had a severe injury which really caused adversity for the team.
